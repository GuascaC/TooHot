Too Hot

Stay safe with "Too Hot", an innovative Arduino project designed to alert you when surfaces are dangerously hot or when you’re too close to them.

How It Works:

Temperature Alert: Continuously monitors surface temperature. The LED turns orange when the surface temperature exceeds 30°C.

Proximity Alert: Uses ultrasonic sensors to track your distance from the hot surface. If you get within 5 cm, the LED turns red, and a buzzer sounds.

Key Features:

  • Real-Time Alerts: Provides immediate visual and audible warnings.
  • Dual Sensors: Combines temperature and proximity sensors for enhanced safety.
  • User-Friendly Indicators: Orange light for caution, red light and buzzer for danger.

Components:

  • Arduino Uno
  • Temperature Sensor
  • Ultrasonic Distance Sensor
  • RGB LED
  • Buzzer

"Too Hot" is perfect for kitchens, workshops, and any environment where hot surfaces pose a risk. Build your own safety system and stay protected!
